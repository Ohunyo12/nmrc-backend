Section 7: Default Charges

If the Guarantee crystallises and the Applicant fails to pay any sum due or to become due hereunder, the Applicant shall pay a monthly penal charge on the overdue amount in addition to being charged prevailing interest rate, management and processing fees on the unpaid sum from the date when such payment falls due to the date of payment.

 

SECTION 8:  Interest Capitalisation

If the Guarantee crystallises and any interest payable shall remain unpaid after the day on which the same ought to have been paid, the same shall be added to the general balance of the account and shall bear interest at the rate applicable to this facility.

 

SECTION 9: Expenses

The Applicant agrees that all statutory and professional fees in relation to the perfection of security/collateral including but not limited to consent fees, registration fees, legal fees, consultancy fees, stamp duties, taxes (if any) and any other fee shall automatically be charged/debited to the account of the Applicant.

Also, all costs, out-of-pocket expenses (including transportation) and other fees incurred by the Bank in the processing of this facility, enforcement and recovery of the facility in the event of a crystallisation of the Guarantee shall be charged/debited to the account of the Applicant and the Applicant unconditionally authorises the Bank to debit its account(s) with any such costs and expenses as the Bank may consider necessary to achieve the successful recovery of the sums owing. Such costs and expenses shall form part of the Applicant’s indebtedness to the Bank due for payment.
